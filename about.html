My name is Siddhanta Ghosh and this is my first ever front-end web-development project and also my first-ever self-created website. This project is called CANDOR-ARTS© or Project Uno©. It is a concept contemporary-art portfolio.

NOTE : Now I am going to talk about why I like contemporary art, feel free to skip if not interested.

On the one side I am in awe of the freedom to express. You can literally make art about anything. There is a significant subjectivity that allows viewers to infer their own meaning and take from it what they value. But when is it contemporary? When it is made with the freedom of expression in mind, or simply when it is made in the current milieu. On the other side, contemporary art has constructed a wall around itself. Although we all like to think that contemporary art invites the masses, it is mostly reserved for the exclusive. Why do I say this? A lot of modern art appreciation depends on artists theory. When I say Mona Lisa, you might not know the artist but you have seen it somewhere, same goes for the Arnolfini Wedding, or The Death of Marat, The Statue of David. The reason for this is because the were and still are accessible to the public in a legitimate way. Some modern art achieves this, but most fall short, allowing only a few to discuss it, or have any kind of discourse, aside from that which the artist intends with its rubric.
